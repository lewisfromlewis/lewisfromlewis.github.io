Values

Patients are served best by best care:

We obtain quality and equity by conducting patient centred research on conditions of importance to the patients we serve.

Get the juice from every fruit:

Through embedding research, and respect for the expertise and labour of every person we obtain efficiency and excellence.

Grow your own:

Through collaboration and trust, we take on and nurture all the talents, and we don't fear staff turnover but provide opportunities for the future.
